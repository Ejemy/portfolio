Personal Budgeting Webapp

(Minimum Viable Product)
For the longest time I have wanted to make a budgeting application as I enjoy working with excel and finances. Here I utilized React and hooked it up to MongoDB to save my information when I close the application.
Features:
-Data continually saved to MongoDB. No need to keep clicking "save"!
-Ability to save an "auto transaction". These will automatically be applied on the day specified.
-Auto refresh ability. The budget and savings will "refresh" at the start of a new month. No need to manually input and refresh your budget!

(React, NodeJS, HTML, CSS, Express, MongoDB)
